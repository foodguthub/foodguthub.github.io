Fermentation GutHub

Bacteria Hookups. No Yeasts Attached.

We are a Singapore-based community of food tinkerers and microbial enthusiasts fermenting our way to better guts. We are connected over the Internet of Microbes (IoM) — a bespoke food sharing system designed to support peer exchanges of fermented goods and starter cultures. The IoM comprises of four main components:

  • 1) a Fermentation Bank for free deposits and withdrawals of starter cultures
  • 2) a crowdsourced UnChope Makan Map listing places where locals offer their starters for sharing (In Singlish, "chope" = reserving/not sharing table in food court; "makan" = Malay for eating)
  • 3) a DIY Mother 0.1. starter culture incubator
  • 4) a GutHub Github — a scrapbook of fermentation recipes, tutorials and experiences.
    • Our aim with the IoM is to create a ‘smart’ network of citizens connected over microbial cultures and peer-to-peer fermentation practices, as a socially and environmentally sustainable alternative to top-down IoT utopias and techno-solutionist frauds.

    Fermentation GutHub Ground Rules:

    • Growth - Grow and fork your ferments
    • Sharing - KrautSource everything
    • Reciprocity - Give constructive feedback
    • Community - Nurture the smart IoM (Internet of Microbes) connections
    • Responsibility - Take a good care of all-the-things-fermenting, GutHub is open for & maintained by everyone

    Regular GutHub meetups are organized in the Hackerspace.sg - check our FB group for details.
